Make your apps more distinctive by taking advantage of our various screen types:

  • Forms - Everything you need for general purpose data capture in the field.

  • Listing - Versatile enquiry screens for your data sources.  
    Add user interactions to jump users into Detail or Form screens when they tap on a row in the enquiry.

  • Details - Drill down to see the full information for a selected data source row.

  • Icon Board - Navigational screen type which allows you to build complex user experiences.

  • Mapping - Visualise your data source rows on a full-screen map.

Use the styling features in the ‘App Setup’ page to customise your app's background as well as the title bar background shading, text colours and image.
