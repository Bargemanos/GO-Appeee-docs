Currently our Map screens require that you provide geo coordinates in order to show as pins on a map. So unfortunately, addresses will not work.

The good news is that you can perform bulk geo-coding of addresses through various online services. You can look into using our own Geocoding API: https://www.geodan.com/geodanmaps-for-developers/geosearch-examples/

Once you've converted your addresses into geo coordinates, then you can upload those into our system and get your mapping screen up and running :)
